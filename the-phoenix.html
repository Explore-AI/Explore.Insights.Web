The Phoenix

Our intelligent analytics platform, The Phoenix, connects your multiple data sources into a secure, cloud-based data store where valuable insights and real-time reports are quickly delivered for business value.

  • Centralize data into a secure, cloud-based data warehouse

    We centralize to all of your data sources (eg. QuickBooks, Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Analytics) using the best ETL and data warehouse tools available. The Phoenix will develop an analytics foundation that will form the basis of your analytics capability.

  • Connect pre-built reports to data warehouse

    We will instantly deliver a set of powerful, pre-configured reports for you to work from. These reports are connected to your data warehouse and update as soon as your data does.
