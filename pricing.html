Frequently asked questions

  • Is the first month really free?

    Yes, for The Phoenix (platform access). You will be able to play around with the ETL tool, data warehouse and real-time dashboards connected to your data sources. This trial will help you to determine if the offering would be valuable to you. Additional work by the analytics team will not be included in the free trial, but it will be included and billable with the platform access from month two if you choose to continue.

  • What is the difference between a Standard and a Premium team?

    The Standard team consists of a part-time tech lead (senior data scientist) and a full-time data scientist/engineer, while the Premium team consists of a part-time tech lead (senior data scientist) and two full-time data scientists/engineers. All these team members are post-grads with many years of experience building AI and analytical solutions.

  • Can I get the Standard or Premium plan without the platform?

    Yes, if you have an analytics platform already set up, our teams will work on there to build AI and analytical solutions. The costs for the plans stay the same.

  • What is the average data warehouse and ETL costs per month?

    This varies from use case to use case. But for SMEs the range is usually $100 - $2,000 per month.

  • Will my data be safe and secure?

    Yes, we make sure your data is safe by always keeping it in the secure cloud and use tools in the analytics stack that adhere to all data privacy standards and security guidelines (e.g. GDPR).

  • What data sources are included in The Phoenix (platform) offering?

    We can centralize almost any data source that you will then be able to access via a data warehouse and analytics tools. The sources that you will have instant and real-time analytics on include Salesforce, Hubspot, Facebook Ads, Google Analytics, Quickbooks Online and Sage Business Cloud Accounting.

  • What is the analytics stack used in The Phoenix (platform)?

    Our platform is made up of a data transfer tool (Stitch), a data warehouse tool (Snowflake) and data analytics tools (Power BI and Google Colab Notebook). These tools are currently the top tools in their area, so we aim to give you access to the best possible modern analytics stack out there.

  • What are some of the custom solutions you have built?

    Here are some examples of the custom solutions we have built:

    1. Buyer/seller matching - An tool that matches buyers to sellers of motor vehicles based on their likelihood of approval
    2. Street pricing - A tool that matches buyers to sellers of motor vehicles based on their likelihood of approval
    3. Claims assessor support - A tool that captures pdf invoices digitally and validates it to drastically speed up the claims process
    4. AI Underwriting - A tool (also API) that predicts default probability based on financials, that outperformed human underwriters
    5. Intelligent OCR - A tool that digitizes pdf financial and bank statements, storing the data and providing insights
    6. Predictive Reporting - An automated dashboard showing exposure, risk and expected loss metrics by various dimensions
